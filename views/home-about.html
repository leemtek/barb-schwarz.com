ABOUT BARB SCHWARZ

Barb Schwarz is The Creator of The Home Staging® Concept and Home Staging Industry around the world. Barb is also the Creator of The Accredited Staging Professional® Courses and The ASP® Designations. She is internationally known as a best Selling Author who has written numerous books on Home Staging and Real Estate. Barb is an Award-Winning Accomplished Professional Speaker and has trained Home Staging from the platform for over 22,000 hours.

Barb Schwarz is the Founder of StagedHomes.com, and The Staging University® and the Founder and Chairwoman Emeritus of The International Association of Home Staging Professionals®, IAHSP.com, and IAHSP® Foundation. REALTORS® and Home Stagers know that Barb Schwarz is the Go-To expert that invented and built the Home Staging Industry around the world after teaching over one million REALTORS® and Home Stagers live in her seminar trainings her Home Staging Concepts.

Barb Schwarz Garden

I like to say, ‘Enjoy the Beauty of the Garden of Life Everyday.’ Life is like a Garden and a Garden is like Life. I see it all of the time and we are each an important part of the Garden of Life. As a child I had a garden and loved every minute in it. My Grandparents, especially my Grandmother, taught me ideas I use to this very day in my Garden now. I hope you will enjoy following me on my Face Book site of BarbSchwarzGarden. There I am sharing ideas and pictures about Staging Landscapes all kinds of information about gardening ideas, growing flowers, roses, vegetables, fruits, shrubs, trees and more. Objectively I will share my favorite products, tools, and where to find items in which stores to save you time and ways to save dollars too. I will tell you how do you keep your roses blooming all of the time. How do you keep mold away from plants? How to keep the bugs at bay! How to have your garden or yard look like one from a magazine with flair and innovation that work for Your Style.

Visit me there daily for a moment to brighten your day and pick up quick ideas and tips. I hope you will ‘like’ my BarbSchwarzGarden (link here too) Facebook page and leave a comment for me and an idea also that I can share with others from you too.
